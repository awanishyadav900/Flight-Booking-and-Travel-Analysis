# ✈️ Flight Booking & Travel Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing flight booking data to extract meaningful insights about travel patterns, pricing trends, and customer behavior. The analysis is performed using a dataset of flight bookings and visualized through Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze ticket pricing trends
* Identify popular departure cities
* Understand customer payment preferences
* Segment customers based on booking behavior
* Provide actionable business insights

---

## 📂 Dataset

* File: `flight_bookings_sample.csv`
* Total Records: 250
* Key Features:

  * Booking ID
  * Departure City
  * Destination
  * Ticket Price
  * Payment Method

---

## 📊 Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Data visualization & dashboard creation
* **Python (optional)** – Data analysis
* **Excel/CSV** – Data storage

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Ticket Price Analysis

* Average Price: ~2578
* Price range: 113 – 4985
* Majority bookings fall in the mid-range segment

---

### 🌍 Departure City Trends

* Most frequent departure city: **Mumbai**
* Indicates high travel demand from metro cities

---

### 💳 Payment Methods

* Most used method: **Credit Card**
* Shows preference for digital transactions

---

### 👥 Customer Segmentation

* Budget Travelers (<1500)
* Mid-range Travelers (1500–3500)
* Premium Travelers (>3500)

---
